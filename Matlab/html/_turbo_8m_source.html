2 %> This class represents a turbo encode / decoder.
3 %> It offers methods to encode and to decode data given a Turbo::Structure.
4 %>
5 %> The structure of the parity bits generated by a Turbo object is as follow
6 %>
7 %> | syst | systTail | convOutput1 | tailOutpu1 | convOutput2 | tailOutpu2 | ... |
8 %>
9 %> where syst are the systematic bits, systTail are the tail bit
10 %> added to the msg for termination of the constituents 1, 2, ..., respectively,
11 %> convOutputX and tailOutputX are the output parity of the msg and the tail
12 %> generated by the constituent convolutional code X.
13 %>
14 %> The structure of the extrinsic information
15 %>
16 %> | msg1 | systTail1 | msg2 | systTail2 | ... |
17 %>
18 %> where msgX are the extrinsic msg L-values generated by the constituent code X
19 %> and systTailX are the tail bit added to the msg
20 %> for termination of the constituent X.
